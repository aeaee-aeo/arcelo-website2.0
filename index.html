About Me

Hey, it's nice to meet you,
I'm Althea Lee

A 20-year-old student from Cebu, Philippines. I'm currently in my third year pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering at Cebu Technological University-Danao Campus. As a dedicated student, I'm always eager to expand my skills and knowledge in the ever-evolving field of technology. Problem-solving and innovation excite me, and I aspire to use my education to make meaningful contributions in engineering and technology. Beyond academics, I have a wide range of hobbies that bring balance and joy to my life. I love watching movies and series, especially K-dramas and Japanese shows.
